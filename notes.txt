1. Edit sender email at mail_function.php, edit receiver email at send_notif.php. 
2. For sending email only (can be executed in background), run send_notif.php.
3. For displaying data on browser, run display_only.php.
4. To configure email & related server settings, go to mail_function.php.
5. To modify email template go to email_template.php.
6. To modify email contents go to send_notif.php (find the $email_content variable).
